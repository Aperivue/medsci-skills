**Key Points:**

- Obesity was associated with 4.50-fold higher odds of diabetes (95% CI: 3.23-6.27) compared with normal weight.
- Diabetes prevalence increased across BMI categories: 7.5% (normal), 13.9% (overweight), 19.9% (obese).
- Non-Hispanic Asian participants had the highest adjusted odds among racial/ethnic groups.
