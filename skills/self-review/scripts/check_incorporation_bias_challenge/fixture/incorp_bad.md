# Growth as a predictor of malignancy in pulmonary nodules

## Reference standard

Nodules were classified benign by (b) complete resolution, (c) decrease in
diameter, or (d) size stability on follow-up CT; malignant nodules were confirmed
histologically.

## Results

Growth was strongly associated with malignancy (OR 50.9, 95% CI 5.8–443.6).
