## Scope
JAMA Neurology publishes original research, reviews, and opinion across all areas of clinical neurology and neuroscience. Key topics include cerebrovascular disease, neurodegeneration, movement disorders, epilepsy, neuroimmunology, headache, neuromuscular disease, neuro-oncology, and neuroimaging. The journal emphasizes clinical trials, large observational studies, and diagnostic or prognostic research with direct clinical impact.

## Special Notes
JAMA Neurology is part of the JAMA Network and shares the same editorial policies, structured abstract format, and Key Points box requirement. Manuscripts rejected from JAMA can be transferred. The journal has a dedicated statistical editor review. Neuroimaging and AI studies are published when they demonstrate clear clinical utility or diagnostic value. Strong emphasis on diagnostic accuracy and biomarker studies.
