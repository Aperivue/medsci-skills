## Scope
JAAD publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies across the full spectrum of dermatology, including medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology. It serves as the official journal of the American Academy of Dermatology and prioritizes studies with clinical relevance, novel therapeutics, and diagnostic advances.

## Special Notes
JAAD is the highest-impact clinical dermatology journal and the official publication of the AAD. It has companion journals (JAAD Case Reports, JAAD International) for transferred submissions. The journal accepts AI/deep learning studies in dermatology, particularly for skin lesion classification and dermoscopic image analysis, provided clinical validation is demonstrated.
