## Scope
Diagnostics publishes original research, reviews, and meta-analyses on all aspects of diagnostic methods in medicine. Coverage includes diagnostic imaging (all modalities), laboratory diagnostics, molecular diagnostics, pathology, point-of-care testing, and AI/ML applied to diagnosis. The journal is broad in scope, accepting studies from all medical specialties that focus on diagnostic accuracy, screening, or detection.

## Special Notes
Diagnostics is a full open access journal with rapid peer review (median ~18 days to first decision). It has a higher acceptance rate (~40-50%) compared to society-affiliated journals, making it a realistic target for methodologically sound studies that may not reach the novelty bar of top-tier venues. The journal frequently hosts Special Issues on focused topics — submitting to a relevant Special Issue can increase acceptance likelihood. APC (~2,600 CHF) is required. Some institutions have policies regarding MDPI publications; check before targeting.
