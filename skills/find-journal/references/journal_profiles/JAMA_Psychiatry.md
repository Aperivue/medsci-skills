## Scope
JAMA Psychiatry publishes original research, reviews, and opinion in psychiatry, mental health, behavioral science, and neuroscience. The journal emphasizes clinical and translational studies with broad impact on understanding, diagnosing, and treating psychiatric disorders. It covers mood disorders, psychotic disorders, anxiety, substance use, neurodevelopmental conditions, and the neuroscience underlying mental illness.

## Special Notes
JAMA Psychiatry is one of the highest-impact psychiatry journals. As part of the JAMA Network, it shares the rigorous statistical review process and structured reporting requirements. The journal has strong interest in large-scale genetic/neuroimaging studies, RCTs of novel therapeutics, and digital/AI-based mental health interventions. Manuscripts can be transferred from JAMA.
