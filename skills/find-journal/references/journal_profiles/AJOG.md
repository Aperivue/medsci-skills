## Scope
AJOG publishes original research, reviews, and expert opinions covering the full spectrum of obstetrics, gynecology, and reproductive sciences. It emphasizes high-quality clinical and translational research in maternal-fetal medicine, gynecologic oncology, reproductive endocrinology, and pelvic floor disorders.

## Special Notes
AJOG is one of the two premier journals in OB/GYN (alongside Obstetrics & Gynecology). It has sub-sections including AJOG MFM (a companion journal for maternal-fetal medicine) for transferred papers. The journal favors large cohort studies, RCTs, and systematic reviews with clinical applicability. AJOG has a strong tradition in maternal-fetal medicine and prenatal imaging research.
