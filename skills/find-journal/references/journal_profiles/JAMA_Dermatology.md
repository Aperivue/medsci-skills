## Scope
JAMA Dermatology publishes original research, reviews, and clinical observations in dermatology with emphasis on rigorous methodology, clinical outcomes, and public health relevance. It values studies that advance understanding of skin disease pathogenesis, treatment efficacy, and diagnostic approaches with broad impact.

## Special Notes
JAMA Dermatology is part of the JAMA Network; manuscripts rejected here may be transferred to JAMA Network Open or other JAMA specialty journals. It has a strong statistical review process and structured reporting requirements. The journal actively publishes AI/machine learning studies for skin lesion diagnosis and has been a leading venue for dermatologic AI research validation.
