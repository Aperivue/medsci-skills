## Scope
JAMA Surgery publishes original research, reviews, and opinion in all areas of surgery including general surgery, surgical oncology, vascular surgery, transplant surgery, trauma and acute care surgery, and minimally invasive/robotic surgery. The journal prioritizes large clinical trials, practice-changing outcome studies, and research addressing surgical quality, safety, and health services delivery. AI and imaging studies are accepted when they demonstrate direct impact on surgical decision-making or outcomes.

## Special Notes
JAMA Surgery is part of the JAMA Network. Manuscripts rejected from JAMA can be transferred to JAMA Surgery without reformatting. The journal shares the JAMA Network submission system, statistical review process, and editorial standards. Key Points box is required. Structured abstract (Importance, Objective, Design/Setting/Participants, Interventions, Main Outcomes, Results, Conclusions and Relevance) is mandatory.
