## Scope
European Heart Journal publishes original clinical and translational research in cardiovascular medicine. It emphasizes large multicenter studies, ESC-aligned guideline evidence, novel diagnostic and therapeutic approaches, and cardiovascular epidemiology. Strong preference for European and global multicenter trials. The journal covers the full breadth of cardiology including imaging, interventions, electrophysiology, and prevention.

## Special Notes
EHJ is the flagship journal of the ESC. It publishes ESC Clinical Practice Guidelines and position papers. Sister journals (EHJ - Cardiovascular Imaging, EHJ - Digital Health, EP Europace, etc.) accept transferred manuscripts. The journal has a strong European perspective but publishes globally impactful work. Imaging-focused cardiovascular research may be directed to EHJ - Cardiovascular Imaging.
