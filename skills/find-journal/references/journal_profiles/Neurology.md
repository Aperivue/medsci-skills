## Scope
Neurology is the official journal of the American Academy of Neurology (AAN), publishing original research, reviews, practice guidelines, and clinical/scientific articles across all neurology subspecialties. Coverage includes stroke, epilepsy, movement disorders, dementia, multiple sclerosis, neuromuscular disease, headache, neuro-ophthalmology, and child neurology. The journal serves as the primary platform for AAN practice guidelines and consensus statements.

## Special Notes
Neurology is the most widely read neurology journal globally owing to the AAN membership base. The journal publishes the full range of neurology research from bench-to-bedside. It is particularly strong in practice guidelines, large registry studies, and epidemiological research. Diagnostic accuracy and neuroimaging studies are core content. The journal follows ICMJE recommendations for AI disclosure. Clinical/Scientific Notes (short reports) are a well-utilized article type for smaller studies.
