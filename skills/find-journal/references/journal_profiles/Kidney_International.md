## Scope
Kidney International publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies across the full spectrum of nephrology. Coverage includes acute kidney injury, chronic kidney disease, glomerulonephritis, diabetic kidney disease, polycystic kidney disease, kidney transplantation, dialysis, hypertension, electrolyte disorders, and renal pathology. The journal emphasizes translational and clinical research with global relevance.

## Special Notes
Kidney International is the flagship journal of the ISN and a leading nephrology journal globally. It publishes KDIGO (Kidney Disease: Improving Global Outcomes) guidelines and practice recommendations. Imaging studies are accepted when they advance kidney disease diagnosis or monitoring (renal ultrasound, CT/MRI for renal masses, AI-based kidney segmentation). The journal favors large international cohorts and has rigorous statistical review. Companion journal: Kidney International Reports (fully OA). Competes with JASN (American Society of Nephrology) for top nephrology research.
