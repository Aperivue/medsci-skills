## Scope
Journal of Hepatology publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies on liver disease, biliary disorders, and pancreatic disease. It covers viral hepatitis, metabolic dysfunction-associated steatotic liver disease (MASLD/MAFLD), hepatocellular carcinoma, liver fibrosis and cirrhosis, portal hypertension, autoimmune liver disease, liver transplantation, cholangiocarcinoma, and drug-induced liver injury. The journal emphasizes clinical and translational research with European and international scope.

## Special Notes
Journal of Hepatology is the flagship journal of EASL and the highest-impact hepatology journal globally. It publishes EASL Clinical Practice Guidelines. Imaging studies are accepted when directly advancing liver disease management (LI-RADS, MR elastography, contrast-enhanced ultrasound, AI-based liver imaging). The journal has strong methodological review and favors large European or international multicenter cohorts. Companion journal: JHEP Reports (fully OA). Competes directly with Hepatology (AASLD) for top liver research.
