## Scope
BJS publishes original research across the full spectrum of surgery including general surgery, gastrointestinal surgery, hepatobiliary and pancreatic surgery, breast and endocrine surgery, vascular surgery, and transplant surgery. The journal emphasizes randomized controlled trials, large prospective cohorts, systematic reviews, and meta-analyses that impact clinical practice. Technology and innovation studies are welcomed when they demonstrate measurable patient benefit.

## Special Notes
BJS is one of Europe's leading surgical journals with a strong emphasis on methodological rigor and multinational collaborative studies. The journal is particularly strong in colorectal, HPB, and upper GI surgery. BJS publishes surgical network studies (e.g., EuroSurg, GlobalSurg) and collaborative audit data. AI and imaging studies are accepted when focused on surgical application. Follows ICMJE recommendations for AI disclosure.
