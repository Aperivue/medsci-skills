## Scope
Annals of Emergency Medicine publishes original research, systematic reviews, and clinical policy in emergency medicine. It emphasizes studies on acute care delivery, emergency diagnostics, resuscitation, trauma, toxicology, and health services research in emergency settings. The journal values high-quality methodology and findings with direct implications for ED practice and policy.

Annals of Emergency Medicine is the official journal of ACEP and the top-ranked emergency medicine journal by citation metrics. It has a rigorous statistical review process. Diagnostic accuracy studies and clinical decision instruments are strongly represented. AI/ML studies are considered if clinically relevant to ED workflow.
