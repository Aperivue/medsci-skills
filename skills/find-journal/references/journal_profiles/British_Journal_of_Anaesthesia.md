## Scope
The British Journal of Anaesthesia publishes original research, systematic reviews, and educational material in anaesthesia, critical care, pain medicine, and perioperative medicine. It values clinical trials, large observational studies, and mechanistic research with translational potential. The journal has a strong emphasis on evidence-based perioperative practice and patient outcomes.
