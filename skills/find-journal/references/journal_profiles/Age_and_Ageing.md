## Scope
Age and Ageing publishes original research, systematic reviews, and clinical studies on geriatric medicine and gerontology. It covers frailty, falls, dementia, polypharmacy, multimorbidity, sarcopenia, rehabilitation, and comprehensive geriatric assessment. The journal emphasizes clinically relevant research with implications for older adult care delivery and health policy.
