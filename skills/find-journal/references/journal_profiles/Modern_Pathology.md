## Scope
Modern Pathology publishes original research, reviews, and technical advances in anatomic and clinical pathology. It covers diagnostic pathology, molecular pathology, immunohistochemistry, digital pathology, and computational pathology. The journal values studies that advance diagnostic accuracy, prognostication, and translational applications from bench to pathology practice.

## Special Notes
Modern Pathology is the official journal of USCAP and a leading pathology journal emphasizing translational and applied research. Digital and computational pathology (whole slide imaging, AI-based diagnosis) are areas of growing interest. The journal transitioned from Elsevier to Springer Nature in 2023. Studies combining molecular profiling with morphologic assessment are particularly valued. Reporting guidelines for diagnostic accuracy (STARD) and biomarker studies (REMARK) are encouraged.
