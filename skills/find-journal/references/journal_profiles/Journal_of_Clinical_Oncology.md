## Scope
Journal of Clinical Oncology publishes original research, reviews, and clinical advances across all areas of clinical oncology. It prioritizes practice-changing clinical trials, novel therapeutic strategies, biomarker-driven precision medicine, and cancer outcomes research. As the ASCO flagship journal, it emphasizes large-scale trials informing clinical guidelines, translational oncology with immediate clinical relevance, and health services research in cancer care delivery.

## Special Notes
JCO is the flagship journal of ASCO. It has one of the most rigorous statistical review processes among oncology journals — dedicated biostatistician review is mandatory for all accepted manuscripts. Companion journals (JCO Clinical Cancer Informatics, JCO Precision Oncology, JCO Oncology Practice) accept transfers. AI/computational studies accepted if demonstrating clear clinical oncology impact with validated endpoints. Strong preference for phase III trials and large multi-institutional studies.
