## Scope
JAMA Otolaryngology-Head & Neck Surgery publishes original clinical and translational research in otolaryngology, head and neck surgery, and related disciplines. The journal covers hearing and balance disorders, rhinology, laryngology, head and neck oncology, pediatric otolaryngology, facial plastic surgery, and sleep medicine. It prioritizes studies with clear clinical significance and rigorous methodology.
