## Scope
CJASN is a leading clinical journal in nephrology, publishing high-quality clinical trials, observational studies, clinical epidemiology, systematic reviews, and policy analyses. It emphasizes research of direct relevance to nephrologists and their patients.

## Scope Keywords
clinical nephrology, clinical trials, epidemiology, acute kidney injury, chronic kidney disease, hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, kidney transplantation, glomerulonephritis, hypertension, cardiorenal syndrome, health services research, patient outcomes, kidney biopsy, kidney stones

## Special Notes
CJASN (IF ~9.6) focuses exclusively on clinical research and does not publish case reports or basic science. Requires a Significance Statement (≤120 words) and limits display items to 4 figures and 4 tables. AI policy: language editing only, disclose in Methods/Acknowledgments, AI images banned.
