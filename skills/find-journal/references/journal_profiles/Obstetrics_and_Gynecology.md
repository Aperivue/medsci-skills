## Scope
Obstetrics & Gynecology publishes peer-reviewed original research, clinical practice guidelines, and educational content across all areas of obstetrics, gynecology, and women's health. As the official journal of ACOG, it prioritizes studies with direct clinical relevance that inform practice patterns, clinical guidelines, and women's health policy.

## Special Notes
Known as the "Green Journal" due to its distinctive cover color, this is the official publication of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG). It publishes ACOG Practice Bulletins and Committee Opinions that serve as clinical guidelines for US OB/GYN practice. The journal has rigorous statistical review requirements and values practical clinical applicability over basic science. It favors studies addressing health disparities and patient safety in women's health.
