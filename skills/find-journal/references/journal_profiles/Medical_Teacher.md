## Scope
Medical Teacher publishes research, reviews, and practical guides on all aspects of medical and health professions education. Coverage includes curriculum design, teaching methods, assessment, simulation, e-learning, faculty development, and interprofessional education. The journal is known for its AMEE Guides—comprehensive practical resources for educators.

## Special Notes
Medical Teacher is the official journal of AMEE and a top-3 medical education journal. Its "Twelve Tips" format is a distinctive practical article type widely cited in medical education. AMEE Guides are definitive educational resources commissioned by the editorial team. The journal favors international perspectives and multi-institutional studies. AI in medical education (e.g., chatbot tutoring, automated assessment, adaptive learning) is a growing area of interest.
