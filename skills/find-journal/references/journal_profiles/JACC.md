## Scope
JACC publishes original research, reviews, and expert consensus documents across the full spectrum of cardiovascular medicine. It emphasizes practice-changing clinical trials, cardiovascular imaging innovations, interventional cardiology outcomes, structural heart disease, and translational science. As the ACC flagship journal, it prioritizes evidence informing ACC/AHA guidelines and clinical decision-making.

## Special Notes
JACC is the flagship journal of the ACC. The JACC family includes JACC: Cardiovascular Imaging, JACC: Cardiovascular Interventions, JACC: Clinical Electrophysiology, JACC: Heart Failure, and JACC: Advances. Transfer system allows rejected manuscripts to be redirected to family journals. JACC: Cardiovascular Imaging is a top destination for cardiac imaging AI studies. Strong emphasis on clinical relevance and guideline-informing evidence.
