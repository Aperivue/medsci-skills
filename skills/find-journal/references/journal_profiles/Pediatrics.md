## Scope
Pediatrics publishes original research, reviews, and policy statements covering the full breadth of pediatric medicine, from neonatal care to adolescent health. It serves as the flagship journal of the AAP and emphasizes clinically applicable research, health services, and evidence informing pediatric practice and policy.

## Special Notes
Pediatrics is the official journal of the AAP and publishes AAP policy statements and clinical reports that directly influence pediatric practice in the US. It has a broad clinical scope and welcomes quality improvement and health services research alongside traditional clinical studies. The journal also accepts systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and studies addressing health equity and social determinants of child health.
