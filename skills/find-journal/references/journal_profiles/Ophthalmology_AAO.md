## Scope
Ophthalmology is the official journal of the American Academy of Ophthalmology, publishing original clinical and basic science research across the full spectrum of eye disease, visual science, and ophthalmic surgery. It prioritizes studies with direct clinical applicability, large multicenter trials, and work that advances the diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of ocular conditions.
