## Scope
Annals of Oncology publishes original research, reviews, and ESMO Clinical Practice Guidelines across all areas of medical oncology. It prioritizes practice-changing clinical trials, translational research with therapeutic implications, precision medicine advances, and immuno-oncology breakthroughs. As the ESMO flagship journal, it emphasizes evidence informing European and global oncology guidelines, drug development, and novel treatment strategies.

## Special Notes
Annals of Oncology is the flagship journal of ESMO and has the highest impact factor among oncology journals. It publishes ESMO Clinical Practice Guidelines and consensus statements. The journal has rapidly risen in impact driven by landmark immunotherapy trials and precision oncology publications. Strong European representation but publishes globally. AI and computational oncology studies accepted if demonstrating direct therapeutic or diagnostic clinical impact. Companion: ESMO Open (open access).
