## Scope
JAMA Cardiology publishes original research, reviews, and clinical insights in cardiovascular medicine. It emphasizes studies with broad clinical relevance, rigorous methodology, and findings that influence cardiology practice. As part of the JAMA Network, it shares JAMA's commitment to statistical rigor, structured reporting, and clinical decision impact. Accepts diagnostic accuracy and AI studies if demonstrating clear cardiovascular clinical utility.

## Special Notes
JAMA Cardiology is part of the JAMA Network and shares its submission system. Rejected manuscripts from JAMA can be transferred here. Requires JAMA-style structured abstract with "Importance" heading and Key Points box. Strong statistical review process inherited from JAMA. AI and imaging studies are welcome but must demonstrate clinical endpoint relevance beyond technical performance.

## AI Writing Disclosure Policy
- **Requirement level:** Required
- **Permitted scope:** Language editing only — AI/LLM tools may be used for editing, grammar, and language improvement; cannot be listed as authors; must not generate scientific content or conclusions
- **Disclosure location:** Methods + Cover letter (JAMA Network policy)
- **AI-generated images:** Discouraged unless part of formal research; must be disclosed
