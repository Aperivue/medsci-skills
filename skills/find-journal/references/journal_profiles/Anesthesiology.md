## Scope
Anesthesiology publishes original research, reviews, and editorials across all areas of anesthesiology, perioperative medicine, critical care, and pain medicine. It values mechanistic studies, large clinical trials, and translational research bridging basic science and clinical practice. The journal emphasizes patient safety, outcomes research, and pharmacology.

## Special Notes
Anesthesiology is the flagship journal of the ASA and among the highest-impact specialty journals in its field. It has a strong tradition of publishing landmark trials in perioperative outcomes and pain management. The journal welcomes AI/ML research related to anesthesia monitoring, predictive analytics, and clinical decision support. Statistical rigor is emphasized with dedicated biostatistical review.
