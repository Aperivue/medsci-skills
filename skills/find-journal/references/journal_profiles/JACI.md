## Scope
JACI publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies in allergy, asthma, and clinical immunology. It covers mechanisms of allergic disease, immunotherapy, drug allergy, food allergy, atopic dermatitis, immunodeficiency, and translational immunology. The journal values mechanistic studies linking bench findings to clinical phenotypes.

## Special Notes
JACI is the highest-impact allergy/immunology journal and the official journal of AAAAI. It has a companion journal (JACI: In Practice) for clinically focused studies. JACI emphasizes mechanistic depth—purely descriptive clinical studies without mechanistic insights are often redirected. Large-scale GWAS, multi-omics, and biomarker studies are well represented. AI applications in allergy phenotyping and prediction are of emerging interest.
