## Scope
JAMA Ophthalmology publishes original clinical and translational research in all areas of ophthalmology and visual science. The journal emphasizes studies with strong clinical relevance, diagnostic innovation, surgical advances, and population health approaches to eye disease. As a JAMA Network journal, it values methodological rigor and structured reporting.
