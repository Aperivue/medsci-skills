## Scope
Circulation publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies across the full spectrum of cardiovascular medicine. It prioritizes large-scale clinical trials, translational science with cardiovascular relevance, novel imaging and interventional techniques, and epidemiological studies informing heart disease prevention and treatment. As the AHA flagship journal, it emphasizes studies shaping guidelines and clinical practice.

## Special Notes
Circulation is the flagship journal of the AHA. It publishes AHA/ACC joint guidelines and scientific statements. Companion journals (Circulation: Cardiovascular Imaging, Circulation: Arrhythmia and Electrophysiology, Circulation: Heart Failure, etc.) accept transfers from rejected submissions. Strong statistical and methodological review. AI studies accepted if demonstrating clinical cardiovascular endpoints.
