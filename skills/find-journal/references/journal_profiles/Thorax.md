## Scope
Thorax publishes original research, reviews, and editorials across the full range of respiratory medicine including asthma, COPD, interstitial lung disease, lung cancer, pleural disease, respiratory infections, cystic fibrosis, pulmonary vascular disease, sleep-disordered breathing, and interventional pulmonology. The journal emphasizes clinical research, translational science, and epidemiology with direct relevance to respiratory practice.

## Special Notes
Thorax is the official journal of the British Thoracic Society (BTS) and a BMJ Group journal. It is particularly strong in COPD, asthma, ILD, and lung cancer research. Chest imaging studies (CT screening, lung nodule AI, COPD quantification) are well within scope. The journal publishes BTS clinical guidelines. AI studies with clear respiratory clinical application are accepted. Follows BMJ Group AI policy — all AI uses permitted with mandatory disclosure.
