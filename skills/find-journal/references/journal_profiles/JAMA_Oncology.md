## Scope
JAMA Oncology publishes original research, reviews, and clinical insights in medical, surgical, and radiation oncology. It emphasizes studies with broad clinical relevance, rigorous methodology, and findings that influence oncology practice. As part of the JAMA Network, it shares JAMA's commitment to statistical rigor, structured reporting, and clinical decision impact. Accepts diagnostic, prognostic, and AI studies if demonstrating clear clinical utility in cancer care.

## Special Notes
JAMA Oncology is part of the JAMA Network and shares its submission system. Rejected manuscripts from JAMA can be transferred here. Requires JAMA-style structured abstract with "Importance" heading and Key Points box. Strong statistical review process inherited from JAMA. The journal bridges the gap between high-impact general oncology (JCO, Lancet Oncology) and subspecialty publications. AI studies require clinical oncology endpoints beyond technical metrics.

## AI Writing Disclosure Policy
- **Requirement level:** Required
- **Permitted scope:** Language editing only — AI/LLM tools may be used for editing, grammar, and language improvement; cannot be listed as authors; must not generate scientific content or conclusions
- **Disclosure location:** Methods + Cover letter (JAMA Network policy)
- **AI-generated images:** Discouraged unless part of formal research; must be disclosed
