## Scope
Gastroenterology publishes original research, reviews, and clinical advances across the entire gastrointestinal tract, liver, biliary system, and pancreas. The journal prioritizes clinical and translational research that advances understanding of GI disease mechanisms, diagnostics, and therapeutics. Strong emphasis on large clinical trials, mechanistic studies, and studies influencing AGA guidelines.

## Special Notes
Gastroenterology is the flagship journal of the AGA and the highest-impact clinical GI journal in North America. It publishes AGA Clinical Practice Guidelines and Technical Reviews. AI and imaging studies (CT colonography, MR enterography, AI-assisted endoscopy) are accepted when they address clinical GI questions with substantial sample sizes. The journal favors multicenter studies and has a rigorous statistical review process. Companion journals (Gastroenterology & Hepatology, Cellular and Molecular Gastroenterology and Hepatology) accept transfers.
