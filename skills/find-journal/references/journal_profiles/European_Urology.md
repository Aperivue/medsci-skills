## Scope
European Urology is the official journal of the European Association of Urology, publishing high-impact original research, reviews, and editorials across the full spectrum of urology. It emphasizes oncologic urology (prostate, bladder, kidney cancers), functional urology, stone disease, andrology, and urologic surgery. The journal prioritizes large-scale clinical trials, systematic reviews, and studies that shape clinical guidelines.
