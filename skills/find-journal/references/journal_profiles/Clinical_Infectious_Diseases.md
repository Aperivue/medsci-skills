## Scope
Clinical Infectious Diseases publishes clinically relevant research on the pathogenesis, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of infectious diseases. It emphasizes practical clinical applications, guideline-informing evidence, and translational studies bridging microbiology and patient care.

## Special Notes
CID is the official journal of the Infectious Diseases Society of America (IDSA) and is the primary venue for IDSA clinical practice guidelines. It favors studies with direct clinical applicability, including treatment outcomes, diagnostic accuracy, and antimicrobial stewardship interventions. Strong statistical methodology and adequate sample sizes are expected.
