## Scope
Diabetes Care publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies focused on diabetes care and management. Coverage includes diabetes epidemiology, clinical trials, translational research, nutrition, psychosocial research, education, health care delivery, and cardiovascular and other complications of diabetes. The journal emphasizes clinical research that directly improves patient outcomes and informs ADA Standards of Care.

## Special Notes
Diabetes Care is the leading clinical diabetes journal and the official journal of the ADA. It publishes the annual ADA Standards of Medical Care in Diabetes and Consensus Reports. The journal prioritizes clinical outcomes research, technology evaluation (CGM, insulin pumps, AI-based decision support), and prevention studies. AI studies in diabetes (retinopathy screening, predictive models, automated insulin delivery) are increasingly published. Companion journals: Diabetes (basic science), Diabetes Spectrum, Clinical Diabetes.
