## Scope
Annals of the Rheumatic Diseases publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies in rheumatology and musculoskeletal diseases. Coverage includes rheumatoid arthritis, systemic lupus erythematosus, spondyloarthritis, psoriatic arthritis, osteoarthritis, gout, vasculitis, connective tissue diseases, and autoinflammatory diseases. The journal emphasizes clinical and translational research, large registries, and studies informing EULAR recommendations.

## Special Notes
Annals of the Rheumatic Diseases is the official journal of EULAR and the highest-impact rheumatology journal globally. It publishes EULAR Recommendations and Points to Consider documents. Imaging studies (musculoskeletal ultrasound, MRI scoring systems, AI-based joint assessment) are accepted when they address clinical rheumatology questions. The journal favors large multicenter European/international cohorts and registry-based studies. Competes with Arthritis & Rheumatology (ACR) for top rheumatology research.
